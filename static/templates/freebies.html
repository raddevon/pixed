Pixed Teacher Toolkit

Updated December 2012

The Pixed Teacher Toolkit provides product recommendations for educators looking to maximize technology in the classroom. In addition to helping you decide what to buy in many categories like desktops, laptops, tablets, and printers, the Teacher Toolkit also recommends some great software and indispensable Internet services to streamline the classroom. It's the perfect starter kit for the Pixed newsletter!

I've filled the Teacher Toolkit with personal recommendations of items that have made the biggest impact in my district plus recommendations for teachers with additional needs outside what we have encountered.

The Pixed Teacher Toolkit is a free ebook in PDF format. It works great on a tablet!
